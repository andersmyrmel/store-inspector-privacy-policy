Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 22, 2025

Overview

Store Inspector is a Chrome extension that analyzes Shopify stores locally in your browser. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

TL;DR: Store Inspector collects ZERO user data. Everything happens locally in your browser.

Data Collection

Store Inspector collects ZERO user data. We do not:

  • Track your browsing history
  • Collect personal information
  • Transmit any data to external servers
  • Use analytics or telemetry
  • Share data with third parties
  • Store cookies or use tracking technologies

How It Works

All analysis happens locally in your browser:

  • The extension reads publicly available Shopify store information from web pages you visit
  • Analysis results are displayed only to you in the extension popup
  • Saved stores (maximum 5) are stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API
  • No data ever leaves your device or is transmitted to any server

Permissions Explained

Store Inspector requests the following permissions:

storage: Allows you to save up to 5 store analyses locally on your device for quick reference. This data never leaves your computer.

activeTab: Grants access to the current tab when you click the extension icon, enabling detection of Shopify themes, apps, and pixels on the page you're viewing.

host_permissions: Enables the extension to scan web pages for Shopify-specific information. All scanning happens locally in your browser with no external data transmission.

Third-Party Services

Store Inspector does not use any third-party services, analytics platforms, or external APIs. The extension is completely self-contained and operates 100% locally.

Data Security

Since we don't collect or transmit any data, there are no security concerns related to data breaches or unauthorized access. All information analyzed by Store Inspector remains on your device under your control.

Children's Privacy

Store Inspector does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13. Our extension operates entirely locally and does not collect any user information.

Changes to This Policy

We will update this privacy policy if our data practices change in future versions. Any changes will be reflected with an updated effective date. We recommend checking this page periodically if you have concerns.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect any personal data, there is no data to access, modify, or delete from our servers. You maintain full control over any locally stored data by managing your Chrome extension settings or uninstalling the extension.
